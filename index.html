About

Our journey to becoming Nairobi's leading digital agency.

  • November 2022

    Our Humble Beginnings

    PM Digital was founded in Nairobi with a vision to transform digital marketing for African businesses, starting with local MSMBs and expanding across the region.

  • March 2023

    Transition to Full Service

    We expanded our services to include comprehensive digital solutions, from web development to full-scale digital marketing campaigns for major brands across Kenya.

  • April 2024

    PM Institute is Born

    Recognizing the need for digital skills development in underserved communities, we launched PM Institute to train youth from slums with digital skills, reducing crime rates and creating employment pathways.

  • May 2025

    PM Gigs Platform Launch

    We launched PM Gigs to place trained students who didn't get job placements, teaching them how to get clients on Fiverr, Upwork and connecting them with clients looking for affordable, quality digital services.
